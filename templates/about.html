About Career Path Finder

Learn about our mission, team, and the technology behind our CV analysis tool

Our Story

Career Path Finder was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to help job seekers navigate the complex and ever-changing job market by leveraging the power of AI and data analysis.

Our team of career experts, data scientists, and software engineers has developed a sophisticated algorithm that analyzes your CV, identifies your skills, and matches them with the most relevant job opportunities.

We understand that finding the right career path can be challenging, especially in today's fast-paced and competitive job market. That's why we created a tool that not only helps you identify suitable job roles based on your current skills but also guides you on what additional skills you need to acquire to improve your employability.

Since our launch, we've helped thousands of job seekers find their ideal career paths and improve their chances of landing their dream jobs.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us to help job seekers find their ideal career paths

Our Mission

To empower job seekers with data-driven insights and personalized recommendations that help them make informed career decisions and achieve their professional goals.

Our Vision

To create a world where everyone has access to the tools and information they need to find fulfilling work that matches their skills, interests, and values.

Our Values

Innovation: We continuously improve our algorithms and tools to provide the most accurate and helpful recommendations.

Accessibility: We believe career guidance should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or resources.

Empowerment: We empower individuals to take control of their career development through personalized insights and guidance.
